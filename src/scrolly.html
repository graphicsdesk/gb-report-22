349 sexual assault and other gender-based misconduct reports were made against known, affiliated respondents in the 2021-22 school year.

During the 2019-20 school year, 144 such reports were made. Last year, that number dropped to 44.

Similar trends arise when looking at the total number of reports related to sexual assault or Title IX violations.

In the 2021-22 academic year, 147 such reports were made. In 2020-2021, 62 reports. In 2019-2020, 83 reports.

94 of these aforementioned reports are still subjects of ongoing investigations, and many more remain unresolved.
